Pinoy Programmers League is an online non-profit organization for professional programmers that aims to promote excellence, global-awareness, intelligence, passion and commitment of Filipinos to the development of IT and programming in the Philippines - through Computing and Engineering; while patronizing the effectiveness of computer programs towards a better and fast-changing world.
